The problem

Your backtest lies.

Not on purpose. A curve that climbs cleanly is the single most convincing thing in trading — and the easiest to fake by accident. Three quiet killers do most of the damage.

Survivorship

You test on the assets and periods that made it to today. The blow-ups that quietly delisted never entered your data — so your edge looks safer than the world ever was.

Overfitting

Tune enough parameters and you stop modelling the market and start memorising the noise. The result is gorgeous in-sample and helpless the moment new data arrives.

Multiple testing

Try 200 ideas and one will look brilliant by pure luck. Without a penalty for how many you tried, the winner you fell in love with is often just the loudest coin flip.

The chart feels like proof. It isn't. Real money is where the illusion gets billed to you.
